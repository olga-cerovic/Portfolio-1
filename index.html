About me

After high school I enrolled Graphic Engineering and Design studies where I gained solid knowledge about digital and print design.

I moved to Norway I had an idea that I could combine that knowledge with programming and that's how I decided to study Front end Development at Noroff School of Technology and Digital Media in Oslo.

I am currently at the end of my first year of studies and I'm looking forward to learning new things and expanding my knowledge in both programming and design field.

I am excited about building my career as a developer and designer and I'm looking forward to new challenges and opportunities.
